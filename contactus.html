Frequently Asked Questions

What services does HospiLink offer?

HospiLink provides integrated healthcare connectivity solutions for hospitals, clinics, and medical practices. Our services include patient record management, appointment scheduling systems, and secure medical data transfer platforms.

How quickly can we implement HospiLink at our facility?

Implementation timelines vary based on the size of your facility and specific requirements. Typically, our systems can be fully operational within 2-4 weeks of contract approval. Our team works closely with your staff to ensure a smooth transition.

Is HospiLink HIPAA compliant?

Yes, HospiLink is fully HIPAA compliant. We maintain the highest standards of data security and patient confidentiality across all our products and services. Our systems undergo regular security audits and updates.

Do you offer customer support after implementation?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 technical support for all our clients. Additionally, we offer regular training sessions, system updates, and dedicated account managers to address any ongoing needs or questions.
